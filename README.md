## Core Strategy

### Quality Over Quantity

- **2–3 meaningful contributions per project**, not spray-and-pray PRs across ten repos
- Each PR demonstrates deep understanding of the codebase, testing, and project philosophy
- Reviewers should recognize your name and style by April

### Genuine Community Engagement

- Participate in discussions; answer other contributors' questions
- Attend office hours, community calls, or async discussions if available
- Show interest in the project's mission, not just the GSoC stipend

### Strategic Focus

1. **Accord Project:** Primary target — smallest scope, best understood, fastest path to quality contributions
2. **dora-rs:** High-impact target — Rust experience valued, dataflow infra is critical
3. **Honeynet/GreedyBear:** Strong secondary — security-focused, growing community
4. **AboutCode & Open Climate Fix:** Flexibility targets — engage if time permits; strong ML/impact angles

### Proposal Differentiation

- Reference your specific PRs and learnings
- Show you've thought about edge cases and scalability
- Align project goals with your long-term interests (systems, security, sustainability)
- Be honest about unknowns; propose learning alongside building
